  • Intro to program and class

    Programming for Games, Web, and Mobile is a VFS program primarily focused on providing students with the tools they need to kickstart their programming careers. It is known for being a highly demanding program with a small number of students. Throughout the program, students have the opportunity to learn development in various environments and programming languages such as C#, C++, HTML5/CSS, and JavaScript, while also gaining experience with common industry pipelines and team management practices.
